    Caterpillar

    The Caterpillar collection consists of a number of classically designed chairs, stools, benches and baskets, all made of abaca bark weaving.

    In the seats, the beautiful, playful texture of the abaca is perfectly combined with elegantly finished reclaimed teak wood, giving the Caterpillar seats that simple yet cozy, warm look. With the Caterpillar collection you can really bring nature inside your home.
